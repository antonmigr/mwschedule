About

Providing massage therapy for maximum wellness

A. Aleynikov, certified massage therapist, has more than 10 years of experience in massage. His goal is using different methods and techniques to find the most efficient and helpful approach to improve body and mind in variety health problems.

This kind of application of massage techniques is able to improve condition of tissues of bones, muscles and joints such as vertebrae, hips, knees, ankles, shoulders and other joints through increasing blood and lymph circulation as well as eliminate toxins from tissues. Massage therapy focuses on release musculoskeleton pain and classical deep tissue massage for relaxation


His long term practice released that this way is useful when people suffer with arthritis, low back pain, sciatica, joint pain and stiffness, cramps into legs as well as sore legs even planter fasciitis and food spurs, chronic intoxication. A massage treatment can relieve tension in the body, calm the mind and nourish the soul, bringing healing on many different levels simultaneously.





Career and education

A. Aleynikov has a strong background in both Eastern and Western medicine as well as massage. He was graduated as a medical doctor from the 1 Moscow Medical University and has PhD degree in classical medicine.
In 1999 A. Aleynikov was graduated from Su-Jok Academy of Eastern medicine in Moscow, Russia
Beeing in Michigan he was graduated from Michigan Institute of Therapeutic Massage and Applied Science and started career as massage therapist in 2003. Membership in American Massage Therapy Association since 2011.
